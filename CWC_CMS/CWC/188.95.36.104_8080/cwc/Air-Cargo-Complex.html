Air Cargo Complex

Ambitious expansion of CWC over the years has also brought CWC in the operation of Aircargo Complexes which is a major step towards providing complete services as a multi-modal transport operator.

Presently, CWC is operating 3 Aircargo Complexes at the International Airports of Amritsar, Goa, Singanallur and Virugambakkam besides managing the accompanied/mishandled cargo warehouse at Indira Gandhi International Airport at New Delhi.
