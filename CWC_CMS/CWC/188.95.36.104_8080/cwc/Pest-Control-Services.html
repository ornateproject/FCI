Pest Control Services

Govt.  of  India, vide notification dated 23rd March 1968 , entrusted  additional  responsibility  to CWC  to  undertake  Pest  Control  Services  beyond  its  warehouses.

Central Warehousing Corporation  is  ISO Certified  under QMS , EMS  & OHSAS .

Segments  of  Pest  Control  Services:-

  1. Dis -infestation  Services – Spraying, baiting , fogging , dusting  activities  to  manage  house hold  insects , pests and rodents  in  residents , hotels , hospitals , corporate houses , institutes  etc.
  2. Fumigation  Services – Release  of  toxic  fumes  to  control  insect pests  and  other  vertebrate  pests, both  for  domestic  and  quarantine  purposes in  godowns, mills, aircrafts ,ships, exports & import  containers etc.
  3. Anti-termite  Treatment -  Pre  and  post  construction  treatment  to manage  termite  infestation  in  buildings. Besides  timber, wooden furniture  & fixture  treatments.
  4. Weed & Vector  Management – Management  of  herbaceous  weeds  and  disease  causing  organisms  through  integrated  pest  management  tactics  for  municipal corporations ,  open  areas and  public  places.
  5. Disinfection – Treatment  against  micro-organism , infection  in  bakeries , laboratories, kitchens  etc.

Accreditation  of  fumigation  Operators:-

CWC  has  taken lead  in  accreditation  of  its  pest  control  operators  under  National Standers on Phytosanitary Measures  NSPM 11 & 12  to  facilitate  MBr fumigation  treatment  of  export/import cargo carrying  wood  packaging  material (WPM) in compliance  to  the  FAO/IPPC  guidelines  issued  through International  Standard  on Phytosanitary Measures ISPM-15.

                 CWC  has  also got its  officials  accredited  under NSPM-22  to  undertake  Aluminium  Phosphide  fumigation  of agriculture  produce  and  other  commodities.

Training  on  Pest  Control  Services :-

        The  corporation  provides trainings  on Pest  Control  Services to  the  employees  and  supervisors , besides various  awareness  program.  Short- term  training programs  for 15 days  are also  organized  by  the  corporations  under  aegis  of  the   Ministry  of  Agriculture.

Major  Customers  for  Pest Control Services  of  CWC :-

Railways & Metro Rails , Airport Authority  of India , Public & Private  Airlines , Institutes , Hospitals like AIIMS, Banks , ITDC hotels, Flour mills , Supermarkets, Food industries, Dairies, Govt. Offices , Port  areas , Oil refineries &  company offices ,  Exporters & Importers.

Service  Site  and Strategic Business Units :-

           The  Pest  Control Services  are  provided  at  all  the  warehouses, ICDs/CFSs/ICPs &  strategic  business  units(Pest Control Cells) of  the corporation .
