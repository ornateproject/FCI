Dear Candidate

 

This has reference to our Advertisement No. CWC/1-Manpower/DR/Rectt/2019-01                       dated 14.02.2019 and subsequent online test and document verification; we are pleased to offer you appointment as ‘Junior Technical Assistant’ in the IDA pay scale of Rs. 10500-28690 (S-9) in Central Warehousing Corporation subject to the following terms and conditions: -

(* The Pay Scale is due for upward revision w.e.f. 01/01/2017)

  1. Your pay will be fixed at the minimum of the pay scale with initial pay of Rs. 10500/- in the pay scale of Rs. 10500-28690 (S-9).
  2. Your appointment in CWC is provisional. Your service conditions will be governed by CWC (Staff) Regulations, 1986 as amended from time to time.
  3. You will be on Probation for a period of one year from the date you join the Corporation. However, your probation period may be further extended depending upon your work, conduct and performance as per the provisions contained in the CWC (Staff) Regulations, 1986 as amended from time to time.
  4. You will be liable to be posted in and transferred to any part of India or abroad.
  5. Your Performance during Probation will be assessed regularly.  If the same is not found satisfactory, your appointment can be terminated at any time.
  6. On your appointment as Junior Superintendent you will not be allowed to apply for outside post for a period of two (02) years. You will also be required to furnish a Surety Bond of    Rs. 10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand only)  in this regard in the Form (available on the website www.cewacor.nic.in) on a Non Judicial Stamp Paper of requisite value as per Stamp Act of the concerned State at the time of joining as Junior Superintendent.
  7.  In addition to the Basic Pay as mentioned above, you will be entitled for Industrial DA (IDA) and other allowances/perks under cafeteria approach as admissible to the employees of your status under the rules of the Corporation from time to time. 
  8.  You will be entitled to HRA/ residential accommodation as per availability and as admissible to the employees of your status as per the rules and regulations in force.
  9. You will be required to undergo such training courses and pass such departmental examinations, as may be required by the Corporation.
  10. You will have to produce the Certificates /documents as mentioned at Annexure ‘A’ (format of joining documents available on website www.cewacor.nic.in) at the time of reporting for joining; failing which you will not be allowed to join CWC.
  11. The certificates of qualifying educational qualification are further subject to verification by the respective issuing authorities and if the verification reveals that the same is false; the services will be terminated without assigning any further reasons and without prejudice to such further action, as may be taken under the provisions of Indian Penal Code for production of false certificate. 

The appointment is provisional. In case of SC/ ST candidates, it is subject to the Caste/ Tribe Certificate being verified through proper channel and if the verification reveals that the claim to belong to Schedule Caste/ Scheduled Tribes, as the case may be, is false; the services will be terminated forthwith without assigning any further reasons and without prejudice to such further action as may be taken under the provision of the Indian Penal Code for production of false certificate. In case of OBC Candidates, if the verification reveals that the claim of the candidate to belong to other backward classes or not to belong to creamy layer is false, his / her services will be terminated forthwith without assigning any further reasons and without prejudice to such further action as may be taken under the provisions of the Indian Penal Code for production of false certificate. In case of EWS candidates, the appointment is subject to the Income and asset certificate being verified through the proper channels and if the verification reveals that the claim to belong to EWS is fake/false the services will be terminated forthwith without assigning any further reasons and without prejudice to such further action as may be taken under the provisions of the Indian Penal Code for production of fake/false certificate.
